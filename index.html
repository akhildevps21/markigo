About Us

About Us

Markigo is on track to help customers achieve their branding goals and thereby grow their business.
Markigo Designs range of services helps you stand out from the crowd and get noticed. Whether it's designing your logo, developing a website, or other designs, Markigo is always with you to help you unleash your full potential.

How We Work
01

Meet

To get a new project underway, we like to meet all client to face to face. Our initial meetings helps us identity and understand your business objectives and figure out how we can help

02

Research

Before starting our work, we like to ensure we understand exactly what we are dealing with. This involves some deep exploring into your company business, your target audience.

03

Concept

Once we understand your business nature, we can begin to explore all the avenues your business can take. This is the stage where no idea is a bad idea.

04

Develop

Once you know what you're after and what direction you want to go in, Markigo will come up with an amazing, more refined idea and present it to you for approval.

05

Launch

This is where the final solution is pieced together, always meeting your requirements and always on time. When you work with the markigo designs you become one of our family so we like to establish a real relationship.

06

Support

Once you become a member of the Markigo family, Markigo will be with you to support all the branding & designs needs of the family members.
